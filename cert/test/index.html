Iridescent Prom

Iridescent Prom is an annual prom held by Seventh Art Club from Nguyen Hue High School for Gifted Students. The event was run under the support of nearly 40 volunteers. Nearly 700 people joined Iridescent Prom on 28/5/2016, at Hapu Place, 81 Vu Trong Phung, Hanoi, Vietnam.
